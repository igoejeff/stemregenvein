Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making healthcare accessible to everyone

 Effective Date: March 25, 2026

Stem Regen Medical is committed to ensuring that our website and services are accessible to all patients, including those with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive digital experience and continually work to improve accessibility in accordance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Our Commitment

We believe every patient deserves equal access to healthcare information and services. Our team is actively working to ensure our website meets or exceeds current accessibility standards by:

  • Using semantic HTML to ensure compatibility with screen readers and assistive technologies
  • Providing descriptive alt text for all images
  • Ensuring sufficient color contrast for text and interactive elements
  • Making all site functionality available via keyboard navigation
  • Using clear, plain-language content throughout the site
  • Providing captions and transcripts for video content where applicable

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some third-party content embedded on our website — such as scheduling forms, maps, and video content — may not fully comply with all accessibility standards. We are working with our third-party providers to improve this wherever possible.

If you experience difficulty accessing any content or functionality on our website, please contact us directly and we will provide the information in an alternative format or assist you by phone.

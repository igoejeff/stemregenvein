Meet Our Dedicated Team

Our physicians, nurse practitioners, physical therapists, and patient coordinators work together to deliver exceptional, compassionate care in New York and New Jersey.

Medical Leadership

Medical Director

Dr. Steven S. Moalemi

Medical Director

A board-certified physician and vein care specialist serving New York and New Jersey, Dr. Moalemi has been board-certified since 1999. As a Fellow of the American Academy of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation (FAAPMR), he emphasizes personalized, minimally invasive treatment plans for patients with varicose veins and chronic venous insufficiency. His patient-first approach combines the latest vein therapies with proven clinical techniques to help patients feel and look their best.

MD FAAPMR Board-Certified 1999 Physiatrist Vein & Vascular Care

BPT MSc Kinesiology Orthopedic Manual Therapy Mulligan & Maitland

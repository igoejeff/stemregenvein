Common Signs You May Need Treatment

  • Bulging, twisted, or rope-like veins
  • Aching, heaviness, or fatigue in the legs
  • Swelling around the ankles or calves
  • Itching, burning, or skin discoloration
  • Restless legs, especially at night
  • Spider veins on thighs, calves, or ankles
